We are pleased to announce that the 5th International Conference of Fluid Flow, Heat and Mass Transfer (FFHMT'18) will be held in Niagara Falls, Canada, on June 7 - 9, 2018 at the Americana Conference Resort, Spa & Waterpark.

The Americana Hotel

The Americana Conference Resort, Spa & Waterpark located in world renowned Niagara Falls Canada; is a welcoming retreat. With over 300,000 square feet the resort features amazing attractions such Waves Indoor Waterpark, Conference Centre and Recreation grounds. The Americana resort has the finest accommodations. All guest rooms come fully equipped. Additionally, the resort counts with quick highway access from either direction coming from Toronto, Ontario or from Buffalo, USA.
